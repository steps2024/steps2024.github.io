第二百八十二阶：我将学习接受在世界中承载内识的责任

发表于2025-01-06|更新于2025-01-13|来自上帝的新讯息第三卷内识进阶

STEP 282 I WILL LEARN TO ACCEPT THE RESPONSIBILITY OF CARRYING KNOWLEDGE IN THE WORLD.

Carrying Knowledge in the world requires responsibility. Your responsibility is to follow Knowledge and to learn to express Knowledge adequately and purposefully. In this, your human abilities will need to be cultivated and elevated. Discernment and all the other qualities of value within yourself will need to be cultivated as well, for you must learn to express that which you carry. You must learn to follow it and become a worthy vehicle for it. This is the true meaning of all individual development. This is where individual development has genuine purpose. This is where your growth and advancement have direction as well.

在世界中承载内识需要承担责任。你的责任是追随内识，并学习如何充分且有目的地表达内识。在这过程中，你需要培养和提升你的各种人类能力。你自己内在的辨别力以及所有其他有价值的品质也需要被培养，因为你必须学会表达你所承载的内识。你必须学会追随它，并成为一个值得托付的载体。这是所有个人发展的真正意义所在。这是个人发展具有真正目的的地方，也是你的成长与进步找到方向的地方。

Therefore, allow yourself to experience the meaning of today’s idea. Allow yourself to accept responsibility. It is not a weight upon your shoulders. It is a rite of passage for you, and in this all things that have confused and frustrated you within yourself will be given a new and purposeful application. Realize that Knowledge carries responsibility. In this, you need to treat it with the seriousness that it requires, and yet with this seriousness, you receive the greatness and the peace that it will render to you. Over time, you will become a very, very finelytuned vehicle for Knowledge in the world. In this, all things that require development will find development, and all things which merely hinder your progress will be relinquished.

因此，允许你自己体验今天这个想法的意义。允许你自己接受责任。这并不是一种压在你肩上的负担，而是你成长的一个通道。在这个过程中，所有曾经让你感到困惑和挫败的事情都会被赋予新的、有意义的应用。要意识到内识承载着责任。对此，你需要以它所要求的严肃态度来对待。然而，伴随着这种严肃性，你也将从中获得伟大与和平。随着时间的推移，你将成为一个非常非常精密的内识载体。在这个过程中，所有需要发展的方面都会得到发展，而所有仅仅阻碍你进步的事物都会被放弃。

In your deeper practices in stillness today, recognize that you have a responsibility to cultivate your faculties of mind as a student of Knowledge. Exercise these responsibilities and do not drift away into imagination. Engage yourself as a student of Knowledge according to the requirements of your preparation, for you are now becoming a person of responsibility and a person of power.

在今天更深层次的静止练习中，认识到你作为内识学生有责任培养你的心灵能力。履行这些责任，不要漂入幻想之中。根据你的准备要求，让你自己全身心地参与到内识的学习中，因为你正在成为一个负责任的人，一个有力量的人。

Practice 282: Two 30-minute practice periods.

练习 282 ：两次 30 分钟的练习时段
