STEP 364 KNOWLEDGE CARRIES ME BECAUSE I AM A STUDENT OF KNOWLEDGE.

As you carry Knowledge, you will feel Knowledge carrying you. You will feel Knowledge guiding you and directing you, preserving you, protecting you from harm, keeping you from difficult and harmful engagements, engaging you with individuals with whom you must be engaged and leading you away from divisive engagements which do not have purpose. Thus it is that you become a leader and a follower, for you follow Knowledge and you lead yourself. You yield to Knowledge, yet you exercise the power of decision upon your own behalf. Thus it is that you become a great follower and a great leader. Thus, you are in a position to serve, and you will increasingly feel as if Knowledge were carrying you through life. And you will feel that you are carrying Knowledge as well. Seen correctly, you will realize your true relationship to Knowledge. You will realize that you carry Knowledge within you and that Knowledge carries your well-being within itself. This is perfectly complementary. It is perfect because it is born of perfection itself.

当你承载内识时，你会感觉到内识正承载着你。你会感觉到内识正引导你，指引你，保护你，使你免受伤害，使你远离困难和有害的交往，使你与你必须交往的人交往，引导你远离没有目的的分裂性交往。因此，你既是领导者，也是追随者，因为你追随内识，也领导你自己。你屈从于内识，同时又代表你自身行使决定权。因此，你既是伟大的追随者，也是伟大的领导者。这样，你就有了服务的位置，你会越来越感觉到，仿佛是内识带着你走过生命。同时，你也会觉得你正在承载内识。正确看待自己，你就会意识到你与内识的真正关系。你会意识到，你的内心承载着内识，而内识本身也承载着你的幸福。这是完美的互补。它是完美的，因为它诞生于完美本身。

Be a true student of Knowledge. Engage yourself in practice. Give yourself in practice. Do not alter your practice. Do not neglect your practice. All you need to do is practice and be attentive, practice and be attentive. Upon the hour and in your two deeper meditation practices, where you come into stillness to be with stillness itself, practice to practice, practice to learn and learn to learn. Today you are learning to learn. Today you are a student of Knowledge.

成为一名真正的内识学生。投身实践。在实践中给予你自己。不要改变你的练习。不要忽视你的练习。你所需要做的就是练习和专注，练习和专注。在每一小时的时间里，在你的两次更深层次的冥想练习中，你都会进入静止状态，与静止本身同在，练习到练习，练习到学习，学习到学习。今天，你在学习中学习。今天，你是内识的学生。

Practice 364: Two 30-minute practice periods. Hourly practice.

练习 364 ： 两次 30 分钟的练习时段。每小时练习。
