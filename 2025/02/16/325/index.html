第三百二十五阶：世界正在融入大社区的世界，因此，我必须保持专注。

发表于2025-02-16|更新于2025-02-24|来自上帝的新讯息第三卷内识进阶
|总字数:1.7k|阅读时长:7分钟

STEP 325 THE WORLD IS EMERGING INTO THE GREATER COMMUNITY OF WORLDS. THEREFORE, I MUST BE ATTENTIVE.

The world is emerging into the Greater Community of worlds. How can you recognize this if you are preoccupied with your own concerns, your own hopes and your own ambitions? How can you recognize what is happening in your world? How can you see those forces that are influencing your outer life and that govern your affairs to such a great extent? Part of becoming strong with Knowledge is becoming attentive. You can only become attentive if your mind is not preoccupied with its own imaginings and fantasies.

世界正在融入大社区的世界。如果你沉浸在你自身的忧虑、希望和野心之中，你如何能够认识到这一点？你如何能够认清世界正在发生的事情？你如何能够看到那些影响你外在生命并在很大程度上主宰你事务的力量？成为内识的强者的一部分就是要变得专注。只有当你的心灵不再被你自身的想象和幻想所占据时，你才能真正专注。

The world is preparing to emerge into the Greater Community of worlds, and this underlies its evolution and all of its advancements now. That is why conflict is erupting in the world, because those who oppose the evolution of the world will fight against it. Those who wish to further the advancement of the world will attempt to fortify the goodness of humanity and the sense that humanity is one community that must nurture and support itself beyond all division of nation, race, religion, culture and tribe. Thus it is that you who are becoming a representative and recipient of Knowledge will fortify peace, unity, understanding and compassion in the world. All this is part of the preparation of the world to emerge into the Greater Community of worlds, because this represents the evolution of the world. This represents Knowledge within the world.

世界正在准备融入大社区的世界，而这正是它进化和所有进步的根本原因。这就是为什么世界上会爆发冲突，因为那些反对世界进化的人会奋力抵抗。那些希望推进世界进步的人将努力巩固人类的善良，并强化这样一种观念：人类是一个整体，必须超越国家、种族、宗教、文化和部落的分裂，相互滋养与支持。因此，作为内识的代表与承载者的你，将在世界上巩固和平、团结、理解和同情心。所有这些都属于世界融入大社区世界的准备过程，因为这代表着世界的进化，也代表着世界中的内识。

Knowledge within the world does not foster conflict in any way. It does not promote hatred or division. It does not promote anything that is divisive or anything that is cruel or destructive. It is the collective experience of Knowledge in the world that moves the world towards union and community. Because your world is part of a Greater Community, it is moving towards union and community because of its own evolution and because it is responding to the Greater Community of which it is a part. You cannot know the importance of this idea unless you can be attentive to the world, and you cannot know the importance of this to you who have come to serve this emergence unless you become attentive to yourself.

世界中的内识不会以任何方式助长冲突，它不会煽动仇恨或分裂，不会促进任何分裂、残酷或破坏性的事情。正是世界内的集体内识体验推动着世界朝向统一与团结。由于你们的世界是大社区世界的一部分，世界正因为它自身的进化以及对其所属大社区世界的回应而朝向统一与团结。除非你能专注于世界，否则你无法知道这个想法的重要性；除非你能专注于你自己，否则你也无法知道这一点对你的重要性，而你来到这里正是为了服务于这种融合。

Remember once again that you can only lose contact with yourself if you re-enter imagination or fantasy, because this is the only alternative to being attentive to yourself and your world. Awake from your dreams, then, and become attentive. Remember upon the hour to look upon the world without judgment, and you will see that the world is attempting to become one community, for it seeks to extend itself into the Greater Community. The Greater Community represents a community that is calling upon humanity to enter and to participate. You cannot understand the mechanism for this, for it is far too great for your eyes and for your mental capacity now, but the movement of this is so obvious and apparent if you will but look.

请再次记住，只有当你重新沉浸于想象或幻想之中，你才会失去与你自己的联系，因为这正是你不专注于你自己和世界的唯一替代选择。因此，从梦中醒来，变得专注。每到整点，请不带评判地观察世界，你会看到世界正试图成为一个整体，因为它渴望向大社区的世界延伸。大社区的世界代表着一个呼唤人类进入并参与其中的社区。你无法理解这个机制，因为它远远超出你当前的视野和心理能力，但如果你愿意观察，这种趋势是显而易见的。

Upon the hour look, and in your deeper meditation practices, actively engage your mind in considering this idea. Today’s practice is not a practice in stillness, but a practice in active and useful engagement of your mind. Consider your own response to today’s idea. Take note of your thoughts for and against it. Take note of your anxieties, especially concerning the world becoming one community in its emergence and participation in the Greater Community. Take note of these things, for here you will understand that within yourself which supports your advancement and that which denies it. As you learn to look upon these things without condemnation but with true objectivity, you will understand why the world is in conflict. You will understand this, and you will not see this with hatred, malice or envy. You will see this with understanding and compassion. This will teach you, then, how you must learn to work in the world so that you may fulfill your purpose here.

每到整点，请观察世界，并在你更深层次的冥想练习中，积极地引导你的心灵去思考这个想法。今天的练习不是练习静止，而是对你的心灵进行积极而有益的运用。思考你对今天这个想法的回应。留意你支持或反对它的想法。特别注意你的焦虑，尤其是关于世界在其融入和参与大社区世界过程中成为一个整体的焦虑。注意这些想法，因为在这里，你会理解你内在那些支持你进步的部分，以及那些阻碍你的部分。当你学会不带谴责，而是以真正客观的态度去审视这些想法时，你会理解世界为何处于冲突之中。你会理解这一点，而不会以仇恨、恶意或嫉妒的眼光来看待世界，而是会以理解和同情的态度去看待。这将教会你如何在世界中工作，以便实现你在这里的目的。

Practice 325: Two 30-minute practice periods. Hourly practice.

练习 325 ：两次 30 分钟练习时段，每小时练习。
